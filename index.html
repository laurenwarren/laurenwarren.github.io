BACKGROUND

Born and raised in North Louisiana, I have been interested in creative hobbies and jobs since kindergarten. I studied graphic design in undergrad at Louisiana Tech and taught myself photography. I was a Production Designer at Romph•Pou Agency after University, but have been solely freelancing for the past year. These experiences brought me perspectives and skills that are still useful to me as a Product Designer. My mission has always been to provide help by understanding needs. By now, I have learned so many skills to do just that.


CURRENTLY

After giving birth to our twin girls in April of 2020, I decided to dedicate my time to growing our creative freelance business, and that has since become my full-time job. I am now looking to switch to a stable, full-time UXUI job.
